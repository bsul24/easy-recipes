SIMPLY THE BEST AVOCADO TOAST

The only avocado toast recipe you'll ever need

I'm sure you've seen plenty online over the last few years about millenials blowing all their money on the fanciest things in life, like (checks notes) ... avocado toast..? Yes, sorry, I think that does add up when you do the math real quick.

Well, irregardless, avocado toast is delicious, even though it gets quite the bad rep from boomers and spiteful millenials alike. But fear not! You can prepare it yourself following the recipe below, and if you do this a few times you'll probably have enough saved for a down payment on a home!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 avocado
  • Slice of any kind of toast (I love Dave's Killer Bread, personally)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dash of hot sauce
  • Mayo (optional)
  • Vegemite/Marmite (optional but highly recommended)
  • Any toppings you like: large slice of tomato lightly salted, fresh lox, hard boiled egg, etc.

Steps:

  1. Place toast in toaster. If using a toaster oven, brush lightly with olive oil.
  2. Meanwhile, mix avocado, hot sauce, and a pinch of salt and pepper. To save on dishes, I usually do this in the avocdo skin, but it's easier in a small bowl.
  3. Once the toast is done, optionally do a light spread of mayo and/or vegemite. Otherwise, spread the avocado mixture out evenly.
  4. Top with your tomato, lox, hardboiled eggs, cucumbers, whatever you have around!
  5. Enjoy!
