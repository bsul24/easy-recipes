HOT OATMEAL

A highly adaptable oat recipe

Oats are your best friend for breakfast, and they're shelf stable, so I recommend stocking up and buying in bulk.

This recipe is one that you can feel free to customize. After all, you are the big deal of your hot oatmeal.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 c. rolled oats
  • 1 c. milk of your choice
  • Chopped fruit of your choice
  • 1 Tbsp honey, maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Place oats, fruit, vanilla, and milk over medium heat in a small pot.
  2. Cook—stirring occasionally—until thick, about 5-8 minutes.
  3. Add honey or maple syrup and a pinch of salt, and taste. Add more sweetener or salt to taste.
  4. Enjoy!
